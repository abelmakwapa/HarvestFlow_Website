The operational reality

Harvest moves fast. Scattered systems slow it down.

Many teams still coordinate harvest work through paper logs, spreadsheets, calls, and disconnected tools. Every handoff — field to bin, bin to packhouse, packhouse to truck — loses a little information along the way.

  • Fragmented records. Pick counts live in notebooks, quality notes in someone's head, orders in another system.
  • Slow visibility. Managers find out about shortfalls and quality issues hours — sometimes days — too late.
  • Costly handoffs. Every re-key and re-count between field, packhouse, and dispatch invites errors.

Harvest Flow is the calm operating layer underneath it all — connecting field activity, crew coordination, quality checks, inventory, and shipment readiness in one place.
